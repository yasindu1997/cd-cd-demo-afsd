What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence is the ability of a computer system to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Learning

AI learns patterns from examples (data) and improves over time.

Reasoning

AI can make decisions using rules, probabilities, and models.

Perception

AI can interpret images, speech, and text (vision + NLP).

Types of AI (simple)

These categories help explain AI capabilities in real life.

Narrow AI

Specialized for one task (e.g., face unlock, spam filtering).

  • Most AI today
  • Fast & accurate on specific tasks

General AI

Human-level intelligence across many tasks (still not achieved).

  • Flexible problem solving
  • Research goal

Superintelligent AI

Smarter than humans in most areas (theoretical).

  • Discussed in ethics & safety
  • Not real today
